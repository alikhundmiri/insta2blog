First Version

The first version of this website will be very simple. Every time you post, we will create a blog post for, on our website.
For example, if your account on instagram is @AliCoderMaker, then your blog posts will be on insta2blog.com/AliCoderMaker.



Custom Markup for the blog will be available for all users.
