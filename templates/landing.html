"Link In Bio"

With aim to help with your mission on Instagram, we have implemented a few features. Link for instance, Link in bio. When your write Link in bio in your caption, those text will become a link on the blog, redirecting your visitors to the link, thats in your bio, at the time of posting on instagram.

Instagram Hashtags or User Mentions.

Credit is due where its due.

If you mention a user or Hashtag in your caption, Insta2blog will automatically convert those mentions to links, which upon clicking will redirect your visitor to the mention's page on Instagram.
